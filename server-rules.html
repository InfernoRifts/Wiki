Age & Content

  • We welcome players of all ages but you must follow Discord and Minecraft TOS (Discord requires 13+).
  • The server is PG-13; adult channels are age-locked and still forbid tasteless NSFW or explicit material.

In-Game Play

  • No hacking, cheating, abusing commands, or duplicating items.
  • No griefing of protected areas or stealing from players' bases; traps, TP-trapping, and AFK attacks are disallowed.
  • Report bugs or exploits to staff immediately ‚Äî exploiting glitches during beta or otherwise is punishable.
  • All builds must be suitable for a general audience ‚Äî explicit, hateful, or excessively violent content will be removed.

Alternate accounts

Players may have one Bedrock account and one Java account. Using alternate accounts for unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.

Farms & grinders

Farms that cause server lag or use duping mechanics are not allowed. Staff may ask you to remove or disable a farm ‚Äî every farm should have a kill switch and be turned off when you are not online.

Examples we do not tolerate: large nether roof gold farms, guardian farms, totem farms using abuse mechanics, and other lag/spawn-hogging builds.

Allowed client-side mods & packs

  • Allowed: HUD mods, minimaps (e.g., Xaero's), performance packs, and non-xray texture packs
  • Litematica is allowed for schematics only (no paste)
  • Not allowed: auto-clickers, Horion client, or any third-party client that gives unfair advantage (like x-ray clients).

Trades & Purchases

  • No scamming through trades, auctions, or misrepresentation of items.
  • Trading or selling in-game items for real money is strictly forbidden.

Players buy at their own discretion. Use trust and the AH 'sell inventory' features to reduce disputes.

Webstore refunds & chargebacks

  • Refund requests must be processed via the owner after a support ticket or direct email to infernoriftsmc@gmail.com.
  • Chargebacks to the payment processor will result in immediate bans on Tebex (we cannot reverse chargebacks).

Punishments & appeals

  • Punishments depend on severity and prior offenses. A three-strike system is used for minor issues, but severe actions can lead to immediate permanent bans.
  • Respect staff decisions while a punishment is in effect. If you believe a punishment was unfair, open a support ticket for a review.
  • Attempting to evade punishment (alternate accounts, books/signs/builds) will escalate your penalty.

Need help or want clarification? Contact staff via the Contact page or open a support ticket on our Discord.

These rules are enforced alongside Discord / Minecraft Terms of Service. Staff have final say in moderation decisions.
