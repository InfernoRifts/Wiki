Basics

  • /help – opens help menu
  • /rules – displays server rules
  • /discord – displays the server's Discord link
  • /geyser offhand – offhand command for bedrock players
  • /sb – toggle sideboard / stats off or on
  • /envoy ignore – temporarily turns off envoy messages
  • /pv – opens basic player vaults menu
  • /pv (number) – open specific vault
  • /skills – opens your Auraskills menu
  • /hopper list – displays all upgradeable hoppers
  • /as – opens customization menu when looking at an armor stand
  • /statistic (name) top – shows top players for a statistic

Warps

  • /spawn – warp to spawn
  • /rtp – random teleport in the wild
  • /back – return to your previous location
  • /warps – list available warps
  • /warp crates – warp to key crates
  • /warp monthlycrate – warp to featured key crate
  • /warp npc – warp to spawn NPC room
  • /warp envoy – warp to Envoy game
  • /warp Fish – warp to spawn fishing area
  • /warp library – warp to library / AFK pool area
  • /warp playershop – warp to playershop area
  • /warp chapel – warp to wedding chapel
  • /warp EndFarm – warp to public End Experience Farm
  • /warp EndPortalRoom – warp to public End Portal
  • /warp pillars – warp to Pillars minigame room

Player Warps

  • /pwarps – opens public player warps menu
  • /pwarp go (name) – warp to a playerwarp
  • /pwarp create (name) – create a new playerwarp
  • /pwarp delete (name) – delete a playerwarp
  • /pwarp info (name) – show info for a playerwarp
  • /pwarp edit (name) – open edit menu for your playerwarp

Lands & Homes

  • /sethome (name) – create a home
  • /delhome (name) – delete a home
  • /home (name) – teleport to a named home
  • /home – teleport to basic home (if you have only one)
  • /lands help – open lands help menu
  • /lands create (name) – create a new claim
  • /lands edit (landname) – edit the specified land
  • /lands rename (newname) – rename your land
  • /lands claim – claim another chunk
  • /lands claim auto – toggle auto-claim while walking
  • /lands delete (landname) – delete a land claim
  • /lands view – show border particles
  • /lands trust (playername) – trust a player to your claim
  • /lands accept – accept a trust invite
  • /lands invites – view current land invites
  • /lands balance (landname) – show land bank balance
  • /lands deposit (amount/all) (landname) – deposit money into a land's bank

Economy & Shops

  • /keyshop – open crate key shop
  • /utilshop – open utility shop (farm minions, hoppers)
  • /shop – open server shop
  • /sellgui – open sell window
  • /sellall inventory – sell all items in inventory
  • /sellall hand – sell items in main hand
  • /sellall (item name) – sell all items by name
  • /ah – open auction house
  • /ah sell (price) – place item in hand on auction
  • /ah sellinventory (price) – list multiple items on auction
  • /qs help – QuickShop help menu
  • /qs create (price) – create a QuickShop from a barrel
  • /qs remove – delete a QuickShop
  • /qs sell – change barrel shop to sell shop
  • /qs buy – change barrel shop to buy shop
  • /qs price – change price for barrel shop
  • /qs item – change item sold by the barrel shop
  • /qs history – view shop transaction history
  • /qs sign (sign type) – change shop sign material
  • /qs find – locate nearby shops
  • /qs fetchmessage – show unread shop messages

Jobs

  • /jobs browse – open jobs menu
  • /jobs join (job name) – join a job
  • /jobs leave (job name) – leave a job
  • /jobs leaveall – leave all jobs
  • /jobs quests – open daily quests menu
  • /jobs archive – show your jobs and levels
  • /jobs info (jobname) (action) – money & exp details for a job action
  • /jobs top (jobname) – show top players in a job

Fishing

  • /fish menu – open fishing menu
  • /fish stats – open fishing stats
  • /fish shop – open menu to sell fish
  • /fish codex – show all fish available
  • /baitshop – open bait/lures shop
  • /augshop – open augment shop

Custom Enchanting

  • /ce – open main custom enchanting menu
  • /ae list – list custom enchants
  • /tinkerer – trade CE books for dust
  • /alchemist – combine two CE books

Custom Pets

  • /pets – opens pets main menu
  • /pets revoke – revoke your current pet

If anything looks incorrect or you'd like additional examples, tell us and we'll update the page.
