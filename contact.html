Disputes & Refunds

If you need to file a dispute or request a refund, please email infernoriftsmc@gmail.com. Our team will review your request and reply as soon as possible.

Game / Support Requests

For in-game or server-related questions (technical issues, bans, gameplay help), open a support ticket in our Discord. This lets staff see context and helps us respond faster.
