Spawners

Pick up, upgrade and configure spawners

Yes — spawners obtained from crates or found naturally can be picked up using a Silk Touch pickaxe. After placing a spawner you can right-click it to open the spawner menu and apply upgrades.

Are there limits?

Yes — to keep server performance healthy we enforce limits per chunk: a maximum of 8 placed spawners per chunk, and a cap of 40 mobs per chunk. Once you exceed these limits mobs will stop spawning.

My spawner isn’t working — help!

Spawner behavior can vary by mob type. Common checks:

  • Check nearby light levels — many mobs need low light.
  • Inspect the blocks under/around the spawner — some mobs require specific spawn conditions (for example, Creepers usually need a few free blocks to spawn and must not be submerged).
  • Ensure your chunk hasn't hit the per-chunk spawner or mob limits.

If you're still having trouble, reach out on Discord or open a support ticket and staff will help debug the specific spawner.

Important commands

  • /spawnertrust add (player) — add a player to your spawner trust list so they can interact with your spawners.
