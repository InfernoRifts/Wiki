Pyrofishing

Pyrofishing — a richer fishing experience

Pyrofishing improves regular fishing on the server with custom fish, rewards, entropy, augments and a fishing totem system. No special setup is needed — just a fishing rod and water!

What makes Pyrofishing special?

As you level and progress you’ll catch many different custom fish and earn in-game money, special items and resources needed for upgrades like totems and augments.

What are crabs & special drops?

Occasionally you will fish up crabs which can drop scales and claws, plus items like dolphin tails and squid tentacles. These resources are used to craft the Fishing Totem and create augments — both boost your fishing in different ways.

How do I sell or use fish?

Use /fish shop to open the sell window and drag/drop custom fish to sell them. Keep higher-tier fish if you want to craft a totem — selling them is permanent, so be careful.

What is Entropy?

Entropy is a Pyrofishing-only resource (like XP). You gain entropy when you fish or use /fish gut. Entropy is used to apply augments and can be withdrawn/stored through /fish augment.

Augments & Augment shop

Augments are boosters you apply to your fishing rod; they can increase money earned, fish rarity, or entropy gain. Buy augments with in-game money at /augshop or find them in Fish Crates. Apply them via /fish augment using entropy.

Fishing Totem

The Fishing Totem is a structure you build (layout shown in the Fish Menu). Activate by hitting the bottom observer with your rod. Totems offer temporary boosts in range and bonuses while you’re in their area.

Important commands

  • /fish menu — Opens the main Pyrofishing menu
  • /fish shop — Opens the shop window to sell your fish
  • /fish gut — Gut fish for entropy
  • /fish scale — Scale for a chance at more money
  • /fish codex — List of available custom fish
  • /fish augment — Apply augments or withdraw stored entropy
  • /baitshop — Opens lure/bait shop
  • /augshop — Opens augment shop

Tips

  • Pair Pyrofishing with the Angler job (/jobs browse) for better rewards.
  • Crabs won’t spawn if you are fishing at spawn.
  • Consider CE (Custom Enchanting) enchants like Auto Reel to boost fishing.
  • Pyrofishing is large in scope — ask on Discord if you need help or want advanced tips.
