Crates & Keys

Crates make the game fun — Keys open rewards

Crates give players the chance to win items, cosmetics and more. You need Crate Keys to open them — here’s how the system works and how to get keys.

What crates are available

At present we run six crate types: Daily, Vote, Fishing, Jobs, Seasonal and Pets. Each crate may contain different rewards and rarities.

Where do I get keys?

Keys can be earned from many sources: Jobs, Daily Quests, the Daily Key Donkey, Fishing Tournaments, the Key Shop, Webstore purchases, or sometimes from opening other crates themselves. New sources may be added over time.

Important commands

  • /warp crates — teleports you to the crates area
  • /warp monthlycrate — teleports you to the featured Seasonal Crate
  • /keyshop — opens the key shop where keys can be purchased
