Jobs & Daily Quests

How Jobs Work

If you’re looking to gain money, jobs are the way to go. By default everyone is entitled to 3 jobs at any time. You’ll gain money by performing tasks specific to each job.

Okay, but how do I become employed?

Good question. To bring up the jobs menu use /jobs browse. From this menu you may see what jobs are available and choose the few that speak to you. You can also leave a job from this same menu. You’ll gain money and crate keys from levelling up every job. Once you’ve accepted a job you’ll start getting daily quests for it, that refresh every 24hrs. Use /jobs quests.

Important Commands

  • /jobs browse - Opens jobs menu
  • /jobs join (name) - Joins a specific job
  • /jobs leave (name) - Leaves a specific job
  • /jobs quests - Opens the daily quest menu

Making Money

There are various ways to make money on Infernorifts. You can join custom jobs, do daily quests, go fishing, sell items to the server shop or make your own player shop and sell items to other players.

Tips

  • ⭐️ If you’re having trouble using the browse menu, try the basic commands to join and leave a job.
  • ⭐️ Some jobs will punish you for certain actions, be sure you know what you’re getting into.
  • ⭐️ Certain quests can only be accepted/become visible at a certain job level.
  • ⭐️ You may gain more jobs by ranking up.
