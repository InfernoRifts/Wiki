Upgradeable Hoppers

Magnetic-style Upgradeable Hoppers

Upgradeable Hoppers are special, remotely-working hoppers that let you collect and filter items in a configurable area — think of them like "Wi‑Fi hoppers" that suck items from a range and send them to configured chests.

Where to get them

Purchase Upgradeable Hoppers from the Utilities Shop: /utilshop. That same shop also sells farming minions which pair well with hoppers.

How they work

  • Hoppers work remotely — you do not need to physically attach them to containers.
  • Open the Hopper Menu by punching a hopper. The menu provides all customization: linking, suction radius, filters, and more.
  • Place the hopper where you want items collected and set the suction range in the Hopper Menu.
  • Each hopper supports up to 10 chests and filters.

Limits & rules

  • Players may own up to 12 hoppers per account.
  • Hoppers that cause lag or use duping mechanics can be removed by staff — always follow staff direction if asked to change your setup.

If you need help setting filters or linking chests, the Hopper Menu is your first stop — if that doesn't help, open a support ticket and a staff member will assist you.
