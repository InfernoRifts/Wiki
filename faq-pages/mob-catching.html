Mob Catching

Capture mobs using special chicken eggs

Found a rare mob far from home? Infernorifts makes catching and transporting mobs simple — with special eggs you can capture most passive and aggressive mobs to collect them as spawn eggs.

How is it done?

It’s easy. Obtain special eggs (buy from players, find in crates, or collect from chicken farms) and throw them at the mob you want to catch. Some tips:

  • Each mob has a different catch rate — you might succeed on the first throw or need many eggs (20+ sometimes).
  • Some mobs require a little distance and accuracy to be caught successfully.
  • When a capture works it produces a spawn egg you can pick up; the mob’s data is retained (e.g., Villagers keep their jobs, cats keep colors).

Things to know / Tips

  • You cannot catch the Ender Dragon or the Wither with these eggs.
  • Chickens cannot be caught with these eggs — you will need to lead them home instead.
  • Baby chickens will not spawn from thrown eggs.
  • Attempting to catch villagers inside iron golem spawn ranges can agitate golems — take care!
  • If an empty spawner mechanic is added in the future, these special eggs will not automatically populate empty spawners.
